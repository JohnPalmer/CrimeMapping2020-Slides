Barcelona Victimization Survey

Which is the most dangerous neighborhood?

  • Ciutat Vella: 30.2% of respondents (\(n=400\)) reported having been victims, during the previous year, of one or more acts that they consider to be crimes.

  • What can we infer about the percentage of victims (defined the same way) in the total population of Ciutat Vella?
  • What is the 95% confidence interval on this estimate?

Point Estimation

$$\hat{\theta} = \bar{x}$$

Confidence Intervals

$$CI_{100\%-\alpha} = \hat{\theta} \pm z_{\alpha/2}\sqrt{\frac{\hat{\theta}(1-\hat{\theta})}{n}}$$
$$CI_{95\%} = .302 \pm z_{2.5\%}\sqrt{\frac{.302(1-.302)}{400}} = .302 \pm 1.96*.023$$
$$CI_{95\%} = \left(.257, .347\right)$$

Qualitative Analysis

Qualitative-Quantitative Divide

  • Words vs. Numbers
  • Inductive vs. Deductive
  • Interpretivist vs. Positivist Epistemology
  • Constructionist vs. Objectivist Ontology

But...

  • One can incorporate numbers into qualitative analysis (and words into quantitative).
  • Both types usually combine induction and deduction.
  • Both include a wide variety of epistemologies and ontologies.
