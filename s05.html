History of Crime Mapping

19th Century: Cartographic School

  • Connected crime statistics, socio-demographic features and cartography
  • Showed uneven spatial distribution of crime

Early 20th Century: Chicago School

  • Burgess and Park's concentric zones model
  • Schematic, rationalized, superficial

Late 20th Century: Environmental Criminology

  • Digitalization
  • Hot-Spots
  • Webmaps
